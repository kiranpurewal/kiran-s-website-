Discover Quebec City: A Historic Gem

Old World Charm

Quebec City is a living museum, boasting cobblestone streets, charming cafes, and centuries-old architecture. Explore the historic heart of the city in Old Quebec, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, where you can wander through narrow streets lined with 17th-century buildings and visit iconic landmarks like the majestic Château Frontenac.

Rich History and Culture

Immerse yourself in Quebec City's rich history and vibrant culture. Step back in time at the Plains of Abraham, where the fate of Canada was decided in a pivotal battle between the French and British empires. Explore the fascinating exhibits at the Musée de la civilisation and learn about Quebec's unique heritage and identity.

Gastronomic Delights

Indulge your senses in Quebec City's culinary scene, renowned for its French-inspired cuisine and local delicacies. Sample mouthwatering pastries at quaint patisseries, savor traditional Québécois dishes like poutine and tourtière, and treat yourself to fine dining at award-winning restaurants overlooking the St. Lawrence River.

Breathtaking Scenery

Experience the natural beauty surrounding Quebec City. Take a leisurely stroll along the picturesque Dufferin Boardwalk and enjoy panoramic views of the river and skyline. Explore the nearby Montmorency Falls, a stunning waterfall that towers higher than Niagara Falls, and embark on outdoor adventures like hiking, biking, and kayaking in the surrounding countryside.

Quebec City captivates visitors with its old-world charm, fascinating history, and breathtaking scenery. Come and discover why Quebec City is a historic gem that continues to enchant travelers from around the world.
